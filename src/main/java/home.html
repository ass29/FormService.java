Welcome to Inventory Manager

Manage your car parts inventory with ease and efficiency using this web app. Whether you're a small automotive business or car enthusiast, this platform is designed to streamline your inventory management. Keep track of stock, insert new parts, delete old parts, update existing ones, and track expenses through detailed statistics.

Seamless Database Insertion

With the click of a button, you can effortlessly add parts to your
inventory and manage your data. With the added functionality
